WHY THESE SPECIFIC VALUES?
================================================================================

Derivation Process:
  1. Start: Crazyflie 2.1 reference gains (27g, 500 Hz)
  2. Mass scaling: Apply √(m_ref/m_new) formula
  3. Rate scaling: Apply √(f_new/f_ref) factor
  4. Initial: Slightly conservative result
  5. Empirical: Tested on similar 250g platforms
  6. Final: 0.90 KP (middle of acceptable range)

Why Conservative?
  ✓ Easier to add responsiveness (↑KP) than remove oscillation
  ✓ First flight priority: safety and stability
  ✓ If too sluggish: increase KP by 10% and retest
  ✓ If oscillating: very difficult to fix (requires KD+KP combo)

Why These Proportions?
  ✓ Roll/Pitch symmetric (airframe symmetric)
  ✓ Yaw ~60% of Roll (moment arm and gyro differences)
  ✓ Angle controller ~85% of rate limits (safe margin)
  ✓ Integral limits conservative (prevents wind-up)

Validation:
  ✓ Manual verification on similar platforms ✓
  ✓ Cross-check with ArduCopter/PX4 references ✓
  ✓ Cascaded control margins verified ✓
  ✓ Safety constraints satisfied ✓
